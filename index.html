About us

Naadathi Furn reckoned as one of the emergent companies of the market and came into existence in the year 1988 as as Sole proprietorship based firm. Our companies headquarter is situated at Chennai. We are prominent manufacturer and trader of Glass Table, Sofa cum bed and many more. These products are highly demanded for their captivating looks and unmatched quality. Naadathi Furn started furniture manufacturing in small scale in Thiruvottiyur. Now located in Manali New Town, Chennai, it covers a wide area for better production of furniture. We are specialised in developement production and sales on multi-category furniture products. With years of manufacturing and technical skills, we are committed to prove high quality and high value-added furniture. Emphasizing the construction of our brand and enterprises culture and keeping innovation as our core concept, we are trying to provide the most competitive and best solutions. With high quality products, competitive pricing and excellent services, we still believe that we get better and better with each passing day.
